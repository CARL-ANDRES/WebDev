URDANETA CITY UNIVERSITY

    Urdaneta City University (UCU) is a public university founded in 1966 by Dr. Pedro T. Orata with the aim of providing basic to higher education in one place for the students of Urdaneta City.

    Together with the team mayor, Amadeo R. Perez Jr., the founders used the proceeds of the town fiesta to establish its first 2-year General Education course using the facilities of the Urdaneta City National High School.
